Data Safety – Blue Guardian

Prepared for Google Play Console

Summary

Blue Guardian collects limited data to provide functionality. Data is not used for advertising or sold to third parties.

Data Collected

  • Personal Info: Email (stored encrypted; deletion supported)
  • Health & Fitness: Sleep, hydration, nutrition, activity (consent required; stored encrypted; deletion supported)
  • User Content: Journal and shift entries (stored encrypted; deletion supported)
  • App Activity & Diagnostics: Usage metrics, crash logs (used for analytics; stored encrypted)

Data Use & Sharing

Data is used for core functionality, analytics and support. Shared only with hosting and crash-reporting providers. No advertising, no tracking across apps.

Security

Encryption in transit and at rest. Access restricted to authorised personnel only.

How to Delete Data

Contact ayorindeo@bournemouth.ac.uk or use in-app delete account functionality (where provided).
